# Digital Literacy Project

##  Overview
This project focuses on improving digital literacy among students by covering essential topics such as online safety, responsible social media use, professional communication, and awareness of cybercrime. It is designed as a practical and interactive assignment to build real-world digital skills.



## Objectives
- To understand the concept of digital literacy  
- To promote safe and responsible use of the internet  
- To develop professional digital communication skills  
- To create awareness about cyber threats and prevention methods  



##  Project Tasks

### Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz
A Google Form quiz was created to assess students' understanding of digital literacy, including topics like online safety, password security, and responsible internet use.



###  Task 2: Professional Email Writing
Two formal emails were written:
- Requesting an assignment deadline extension  
- Applying for a summer internship  

This task focuses on proper email structure and professional communication.



###  Task 3: Responsible Social Media Use Checklist
A checklist of Do’s and Don’ts was created to guide students on safe and ethical behavior while using social media platforms.

###  Task 4: Cybercrime Case Study – Identity Theft
A case study explaining identity theft, including how it happens, who is targeted, and its consequences. This helps in understanding real-world cyber risks.



###  Task 5: Stay Safe Online Checklist
A practical checklist with safety tips for students, including financial/UPI safety and cybercrime reporting methods.

##  Key Learnings
- Importance of online safety and privacy  
- Awareness of cyber threats like phishing and identity theft  
- Professional email writing skills
